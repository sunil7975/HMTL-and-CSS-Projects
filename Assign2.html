Darshan Thoogudeepa (born 16 February 1977) known mononymously as Darshan, is an Indian actor, producer, and distributor who works predominantly in Kannada He began his acting career in soap operas and small films in the mid-1990s. His first film in a lead role was in the 2002 Kannada movie Majestic.
